People in the laboratory

Profa. Dra. Eneida Maria Eskinazi Sant Anna

Online CV | ORCID

Master's degree in Environmental Engineering Sciences from the University of São Paulo (São Carlos School of Engineering) and PhD in Ecology from the University of São Paulo (2000). She is a professor at the Federal University of Ouro Preto, working in the field of Ecosystem and Community Ecology, mainly on the following topics: ecology of continental aquatic ecosystems, impacts of mining on aquatic ecosystems and zooplankton ecology. She has worked on the study of high altitude lakes and lagoons in the Iron Quadrangle. Activities in the area of science communication and environmental education with an emphasis on aquatic ecosystems are also among her areas of interest.
